Welcome to:
City As Studio 2021

>
>
>

Your Brief:

As a City as Studio young artist, you have been commissioned by Modern Art Oxford to produce a collaborative original moving image work in response to our exhibition Ruth Asawa: Citizen of the Universe.

Over the residency you will be encouraged to work independently to explore what makes you a “Citizen of the Universe”. You will be encouraged to amplify your voice and consider the current personal or political concerns that are impacting you today.

The zoom sessions and online workshops will guide you in forming your own visual voice, before coming together as a group to create a final one-minute film. Modern Art Oxford will showcase this film in summer 2021.

>
>

Project Aims:

  • To create original impactful work, question your assumptions, and explore ideas to disrupt and develop your moving image practice.

>

By the end of this project you will know:

  • How professional artists make creative, unique films.
  • What artistic and technical skills go into filmmaking.
  • How art films develop from idea, to reality, to being shown to public audiences.
  • What it is really like to have your art film shown at Modern Art Oxford.
