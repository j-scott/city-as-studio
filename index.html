Welcome to:
City As Studio

>
>
>

Brief:

As a City as Studio young artist, you have been commissioned by Modern Art Oxford to produce a collaborative original moving image work in response to our exhibition Ruth Asawa: Citizen of the Universe.

In the two online workshops you will be encouraged to work independently to explore what makes you a “Citizen of the Universe”. You will be encouraged to amplify your voice and consider the current personal or political concerns that are impacting you today.

These two online workshops will guide you in forming your own visual voice to your ideas, before coming together as a group to create a final one-minute film in workshop three. Modern Art Oxford will showcase this film in summer 2020.

>
>

Project aims:

  • To create an original impactful work, question your assumptions and explore ideas to disrupt and develop your moving image practice.

>

By the end of this project you will know answers to:

  • How professional artists make creative, unique films.
  • What artistic and technical skills go into film making.
  • How art films develop from idea, to reality, to being shown to public audiences.
  • What it is really like to have your new art film shown to an audience by Modern Art Oxford.
