Workshop 2:
Text and Image - Creating Meaning

Intro:

In this workshop you will explore filmmaking and storytelling though image and text, taking inspiration from artists using newspapers, everyday objects and found online content. You will discover how to create new meaning and connections though creative processes and arts activism to consider what it means to be a citizen of the universe.

"Stop Motion" or "Stop motion"

The goal of this workshop will be to produce a Stop Motion film that articulates your message as a "Citizen of the Universe".

As last week, remember to use your Zine to complete your workshop tasks in to build a record of your ideas. (You may find it useful to look over your themes from last week!)

Also remember that we don't expect you to be video professionals yet and this workshop remains a chance for you to experiment and learn. So don't be worried if you aren't totally satisfied with your results today.

In our next and final workshop (Workshop 3) we will be putting what you have learnt over these two workshops into action by creating a final collaborative film!
