Workshop 2:
Text and Image - Creating Meaning

Intro:

In this workshop you will explore filmmaking and storytelling though image and text, taking inspiration from artists using newspapers, everyday objects and found online content. You will discover how to create new meaning and connections though creative processes and arts activism to consider what it means to be a citizen of the universe
